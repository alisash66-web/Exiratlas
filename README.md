# Exir Tejarat Atlas (ETA)

## Overview
Exir Tejarat Atlas (ETA) is an international trading and sourcing company specializing in Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Steel, and Industrial Equipment supply.

We connect global OEM manufacturers with industrial projects in:
- Iran (Primary Market)
- Middle East
- Europe
- China
- India
- Turkey

## Core Business Scope
- Pumps (Centrifugal, Multistage, API 610)
- Compressors (Centrifugal, Reciprocating)
- Valves (Gate, Globe, Check, Control)
- Heat Exchangers
- Rotating Equipment
- Industrial Spare Parts
